Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Heat sugar and make syrup.
  2. Mix bread crumbs and make dough.
  3. Shape into balls and deep fry in hot oil.
  4. Dip the fried balls in syrup and serve warm.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Condensed milk
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee (clarified butter)

Recipe

  1. Cook condensed milk with ghee.
  2. Once it thickens, add cardamom powder.
  3. Let it set, then cut into pieces and serve.

Misal

Ingredients

  • Sprouted moong beans
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (Chili powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala)
  • Poha or Pav (bread)

Recipe

  1. Cook the sprouted moong beans with spices and water.
  2. Sauté onions and tomatoes in oil, and add to the mixture.
  3. Simmer until it thickens, then serve with poha or pav and garnish with coriander.

Wada Pav

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (Chana)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (Chili powder, Garam Masala)

Recipe

  1. Cook chickpeas until soft.
  2. Sauté onions and tomatoes with spices. Add chickpeas and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Garnish with coriander and serve with rice or bread.
