When did MisalPav first appear in India?

MisalPav is believed to have originated in the state of Maharashtra, India, around the early 1900s. It is a traditional spicy dish made of curried lentils (misal) served with pav (bread). The dish gained popularity as a street food, particularly in Mumbai, where it became a beloved breakfast or snack option. The combination of spiced lentils and bread has made MisalPav a staple of Maharashtrian cuisine. (Source: India Today Magazine )
